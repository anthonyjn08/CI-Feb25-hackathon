About the Site

As the footer comments, the aim of the site in to "Create meaningful connections through unique events and experiences". Our aim is to help people find events to enable them to do this. All events have the potential to either create a lasting impact or reignite the spark in an existing relationship. Whether that comes from an intimate workshop, a mixers event or maybe the dance floor at one of the dance classes. The events are inclusive and welcoming, celebrating diversity and making sure that everyone feels valued and also has fun.
